## Core Service
**ScholarshipRecommendationService** — two-stage matching:
1. **Hard filter** — nationality, study_level, income_category, income ceiling, SPM A+/credits, CGPA, field, institution_type, deadline → fail = "Not Suitable" (0 score)
2. **Soft scoring** (0-100): Academic 40 + Field 25 + Institution 20 + Income 15 → status: Eligible (≥70), Partially Eligible (50-69), Not Suitable (<50)
3. Every recommendation includes explainable breakdown + `is_preliminary` flag if results pending
